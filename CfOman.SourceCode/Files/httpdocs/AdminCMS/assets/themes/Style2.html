Corrosion & Leaks In The Oil & Gas

Facts

The global cost of corrosion is estimated to be US$2.5 trillion*
The global oil and gas industries allow as much as 3.6 trillion cubic feet of natural gas to escape into the atmosphere annually*

VALUE DRIVERS

  • Increased Asset Integrity
  • Reduced Cost of Inspection
  • Reduced Cost of Turnarounds
  • Reduced Production Loss
  • Reduced Safety and Environmental Risk

CONSEQUENCES OF POOR ASSET INTEGRITYMANAGEMENT

  • Leaks and equipment failures.
  • Corrosion at unexpected locations.
  • Poor asset integrity performance.
  • Asset downtime.
  • HSE incidents.
  • Cost, lives and reputation at stake.
