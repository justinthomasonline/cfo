Thermographic Inspection

Thermal Imaging Technology been used to assist in the identification of areas of pipeline where internal scaling of the pipeline was occurring and for leak detection as well. Heat in any form will flow from Hot to Cold irrespective of direction, Scale will act as an insulator and as such will slow down the rate at which heat will travel from Hot to Cold.

Challenges on Current thermographic inspection

  • Limited range of detection due to penetrating power.
  • Time consuming and costly.
  • Safety concerns.
  • Need for high level of expertise to interpret results.
  • Difficulties identifying small pitting.

Solution For Overcoming Inspection Challenges By Leveraging

  • Drone Inspection
  • Internet of Things (IOT) & Advance Sensor
  • Advanced Analytics Machine Learning
